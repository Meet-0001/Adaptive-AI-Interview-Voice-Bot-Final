Everything you need to prepare

A complete interview coaching system that runs entirely on your machine.

🎤

Voice Interview

Speak your answers naturally. Whisper transcribes your speech in real-time for a realistic interview simulation.

🔊

AI Voice Coach

Hear your mentor speak via Edge TTS with a natural male voice — Guy Neural. Like having a real coach by your side.

🧠

Local AI (Ollama)

Powered by Llama 3.2 running locally via Ollama. 100% private — your interview data never leaves your machine.

🎯

4 Difficulty Levels

Choose from Very Easy, Easy, Medium, or Difficult. The AI adapts question complexity and evaluation depth accordingly.

🗃️

Smart History

SQLite stores your last 10 interview sessions. Older sessions auto-delete to keep things clean.

⭐

Detailed Scoring

Get scores on Relevance, Clarity, and Depth after every answer — plus strengths, weaknesses, and actionable feedback.
